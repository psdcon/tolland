About us

Operating from Port of Belfast, James Tolland & Co Ltd is a long established and trusted supplier to the agricultural supply trade. We specialise in the supply of feed raw materials to compounders, Feed Blenders and Merchants throughout Ireland.

Working from the quayside of an international port we trade a wide range of feed ingredients including Barley, Wheat, Maize, Wheatfeed, Soya, Corn Gluten, Corn Distillers, Sugarbeet and Fishmeal.

As members of the Northern Ireland Grain Trade Association (NIGTA), Food Fortress Ireland, Organic Food Federation and COCERAL, all goods are quality assured and sourced from certified companies providing full traceability thus giving peace of mind to the end user.

The Food Fortress scheme developed with Queen's University institute for Global Food Security gives NIGTA a world leading position in the field of feed assurance.

Our aim is to provide customers with real time market information so they can react to world market trends and secure best prices for their raw materials.
